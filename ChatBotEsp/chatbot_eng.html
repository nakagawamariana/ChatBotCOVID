All the information provided has been supervised by a team of physicians. In addition, we have relied on widely recognized sources of information.

Technology as a tool against disinformation

Cova has been designed to help you by using Artificial Intelligence algorithms. These algorithms try to predict what kind of information you want to find out by analyzing the question you enter.

Chatbots have been used by countless companies to try to resolve customer queries and reduce the workload of their employees.

Over the last few years, hospitals and clinics have been incorporating this technology into their websites and APPs to try to improve patient care.

No more waiting on the phone, no more searching the internet to find the information you were looking for and no more contradictory messages from different sources. Cova will provide you with the necessary information, supervised by experts, in a single search and in an accessible way.
